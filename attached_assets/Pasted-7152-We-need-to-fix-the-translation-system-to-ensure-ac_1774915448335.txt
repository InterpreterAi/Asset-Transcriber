1) LOCK TRANSLATION AFTER FIRST RESULT

Problem:
Translations are being recomputed after appearing, which causes words and phrasing to change.

Required behavior:

Each segment must be translated exactly once.

Implementation:

When a segment becomes finalized:

segment.translation = result
segment.translationLocked = true

Add a guard condition:

if (segment.translationLocked) return

After translationLocked is true:

• do not send the segment to translation again
• do not overwrite the translation
• do not recompute translation
• do not re-render translation

Translations must remain immutable after first render.
